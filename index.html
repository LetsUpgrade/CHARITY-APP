OUR CAUSES

Emergency

Problems are always uninvited, you never know what’s wrong happens with someone in the very next moment. So, it’s always better to be ready for such situations.

Let’s donate for those who struck in natural calamities or for those who fight for their lives as well as for ours or for many unexpected problems like that.

Individual

A single step of yours can change a life of any individual. Come forward and donate for any cause you that you might think is the most crucial sector that needs help.

You can either donate your time or your money or anything that can help anyone.

Organization

Be a small helping hand or a little live supporting system for those who are alone in this big world.

Donate for organizations who give home to homeless peoples and provide them food, education, health and many other essential items of daily needs to those who can’t afford all these.

About Us

“website-name” is a small initiative of a group of around 10 people across different parts of India getting together just to make a wish came true that we can support our nation to be a better place for everyone. For doing such we create a place where you can donate for any cause that you would like and be a part of our family.
Helping at least a single person may do not change the world but it can change the world for that one person. Helping others is a sweet pleasure which we feel when we do something good for someone.
For making donations just explore our website a bit, and choose for whom you can donate.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is “website-name” and how does it works?

    “website-name” is a non-profit organization which directly connect donor with the one for whom he/she donates.
    Which means there is no third party interference between needy people and people who helps them that is donor. They get direct benifits.

  • What kinds of charities does “website-name” recommend?

    “website-name” recommends three types of charities which is for individual,emergency and organizations.

  • How does charity for individual works?

    In individual charity, you can donate for any cause like for child education, meals for hungry, for any individual’s health issue, etc.

  • How does charity for emergency works?

    In emergency charity, you can donate for any emergency situations like flood, tsunami, any other natural calamities, any medical emergencies like covid-19, ebola, etc.

  • How does charity for organization works?

    In organization charity, you can donate for any organization like anaath ashrams, old age homes, homes for special abled people, etc.

  • How can we pay?

    You can pay using any online mode of payment like Paytm, BHIM app, Mobile Banking and rest you can find after selecting donate option.
